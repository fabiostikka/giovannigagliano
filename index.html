Giovanni Gagliano

Bio

I started singing in bands in 1996 and from there I went through a lot of music, people, adventures, fun... I had great experiences during those years with Soul in cage, White Shadows and Mastif. I also had a great year with the London metal band Hagstone, touring around in UK. After that I started my solo career with 3 albums: Peace & revenge, Rough uneven surface and Restless. All of them contain all my musical background, from grunge to hard rock, from rock to heavy metal.

Albums


Peace & Revenge (2014)

This is my debut album which contains stuff from my past plus new compositions. I consider it a sort of resume of what I did in my career to that point and it was my first experience recording something. Well… considering that, I am very proud of it.

Rough Uneven Surface (2016)

I had no plan to release a second album but after Peace & revenge I had some material left and inspiration for other songs. This one was very quick, I started it in January and in March everything was ready. I consider it a sort of crazy album.

Restless (2017)

At some point I started thinking about new songs, and…why not? This is a sort of concept album about London, where I live, and the majority of the songs are inspired from the London life. I took my time composing it and recording it. People say this is my best album (so far?). Well, probably I agree.
